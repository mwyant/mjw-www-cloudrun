Meetings & Public Notices

Regular Meeting Schedule

  • Town Board: Second Monday of each month, 7:00 PM (Town Hall).
  • Planning Board: First Wednesday of each month, 7:00 PM.
  • Town Court: See the Court page for dates and instructions.

Meeting times can change for holidays or emergencies. Notices are posted here and at Town Hall.

Agendas, Minutes, and Participation

  • Agendas are posted as available prior to meetings.
  • Minutes are archived on the Clerk page when approved.
  • Residents may submit written comments to the Town Clerk.

Privacy

The Town of Warren does not sell personal information and does not serve behavioral ads. We keep only the information needed to respond to inquiries and fulfill requests.

Email is not guaranteed secure; consider mail or phone for sensitive matters.

Accessibility

We aim to make this website usable for everyone, including residents using assistive technology. If you have trouble accessing a document, contact the Town Clerk for an accessible format.

Website Accuracy

Documents are posted for convenience and may be superseded by later filings. For official copies, verification, or historical records, contact the Town Clerk.
