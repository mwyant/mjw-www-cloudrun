Town Court

Justice matters, citations, and local hearings are scheduled and managed by Warren Justice Court. View cases, payment options, and contact information below.

Schedule & Procedures

Court Dates

Court convenes twice monthly for local citations. Call the court clerk for the next calendar.

Payments

Payments accepted at Town Hall or by mail. Bring citation and case number when appearing in person.

Legal Resources

Visit the Herkimer County Courts site or contact the Judge’s clerk for forms.

Accessibility

The courtroom is accessible via the Town Hall entrance. Request accommodations through the Clerk.

Court Contacts

Town Court

Contact the court clerk at Town Hall for scheduling and records.

Phone: (315) 858-1207

Judge’s Office

Presiding judge handles arraignments and sentencing.

Traffic & Codes

Coordinate with Codes & Highway for road-related citations.
