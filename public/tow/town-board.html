Town Board

Five elected officials safeguard budgets, policy, and planning for the Town of Warren. Meetings happen monthly at Warren Town Hall and are open to the public.

Board Focus Areas

Governance & Budgets

Superintendent-led budgets, audits, and grant oversight ensure town finances stay transparent. Board often reviews the fiscal plan ahead of public hearings.

Ordinances & Planning

Board sponsorship of local laws, energy policies, and zoning updates keeps residents informed of land-use direction.

Community Services

Board sets priorities for road maintenance, public safety partnerships, and clerk-led record management.
